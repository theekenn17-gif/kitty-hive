# 🐝 KITTY HIVE

> **Build. Learn. Protect. Improve. Generate. Expand.**

**Kitty Hive** is a modular AI operating system designed to evolve into the central intelligence of the future **KenCorp** ecosystem.

Unlike traditional AI assistants, Kitty is built as a collaborative hive of specialized agents capable of learning, reasoning, improving itself, and eventually operating businesses autonomously.

---

# 👑 Vision

Kitty is being developed to become the executive intelligence behind multiple industries, including:

- ⚽ Sports Intelligence
